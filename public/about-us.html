Our Story

AFounded in 1995,Pizzeria started as a small family-owned pizzeria in the heart of Toronto. Our founder, Group13, grew up in a family where cooking was a way of life. Inspired by the rich culinary traditions of Italy and the vibrant food culture of Toronto, Group13 set out to create a place where people could come together to enjoy delicious, handcrafted pizzas made with love and the finest ingredients.
